What we offer

Everything your farm
needs in one place

From listing your seeds to buying from other farmers, SeedBank connects your community.

🌿
Seed Listings

Post seeds with price and quantity. Your listing goes live instantly and is visible to all farmers on the platform.

🎁
Direct Marketplace

Browse all available seeds from other farmers. Buy instantly with one click — no middlemen, no waiting.

✅
Order History

Every purchase is tracked. See what you bought, from whom, how much, and when — your complete history at a glance.

📈
Admin Oversight

Admins monitor all listings, manage user accounts, and keep the marketplace running smoothly and safely.

🔒
Secure Auth

JWT-based authentication with role separation. Farmers and admins each see only what they need. Always secure.

🌎
Biodiversity

By connecting farmers directly, SeedBank helps preserve rare and traditional varieties that might otherwise be lost.

Simple process

Up and running
in three steps

No complicated setup. Register, list your seeds, and start trading with your community.

Step one
01
Create your account

Register in seconds with just a username and password. Choose your role — farmer or admin — and you're in immediately.

Step two
02
List your seeds

Add the seeds you want to sell. Set a name, category, price per kg, and quantity. Your listing appears instantly on the marketplace.

Step three
03
Buy and sell

Browse seeds from other farmers, click Buy, enter the quantity, confirm — instant purchase. No delays, no approval needed.

Built for everyone

Two roles, one platform

Designed for farmers trading seeds and admins keeping things running.

Farmer
For the farmer

List your seeds, browse the marketplace, buy from other farmers, and track all your purchases in one clean dashboard.

  • Post seeds with price and quantity
  • Edit or remove listings anytime
  • Browse all seeds from other farmers
  • Buy directly with instant confirmation
  • Track your full purchase history
Admin
For the admin

Oversee the entire platform, manage users, and monitor all seed activity across the community.

  • View all seed listings from farmers
  • Manage all registered users
  • Delete farmer accounts when needed
  • Monitor seed request activity
  • Keep the marketplace healthy
